About Me

MA

Mahfooz Ahmad

Application Architect • Microservices • Java & Spring • Tech Educator

I am an Application Architect with over 20 years of experience designing and building enterprise systems using Java, Spring, Kafka, Docker, Kubernetes, and modern cloud platforms. I specialize in microservices, event‑driven architectures, and resilient distributed systems.

Through my SelfTechLearner YouTube channel and this blog, I break down complex concepts in microservices, Spring Boot, WebFlux, and system design into practical, production‑focused content for engineers and architects.
